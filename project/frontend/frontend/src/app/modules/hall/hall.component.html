  1. Children's ticket - a ticket for children under 6 years old inclusively for films 0 +, 6 +. The age of the child is determined visually by the cinema employee. In case of doubt about the age of the child, the cinema employee is entitled to demand the presentation of a document certifying the age of the child.

At the entrance to the hall, the ticket must be presented to the cinema employee together with a pension certificate and / or a certificate of a disabled person, respectively.
